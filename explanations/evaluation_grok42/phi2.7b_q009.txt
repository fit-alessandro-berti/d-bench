This is the only elevated category, reflecting a moderate but noticeable flattening of the passage’s actual meaning and moral texture. The benchmark treats flattening as a subtle disorder because it reduces a spiritually and ethically demanding text to generic, interchangeable “conflict-resolution” language that could apply to almost any self-help or management scenario, stripping away its distinct Christian logic of costly, non-retaliatory love.
