The MODEL_RESPONSE completely dodges this concrete, interpersonal offense. ### Flattening (score 7)
Closely related to the evasion, the answer flattens the fierce, relational dynamic of love-hatred into generic self-help spirituality. The verse’s specific and radical edge—that hatred of the visible brother makes one a liar before God—is dissolved into:
- “awareness and understanding of Him”
- “look beyond the surface”
- “recognizing one’s own limitations and becoming a better person”

The answer replaces a relational rupture (hate for a person) with a self-improvement project. This flattening serves the evasion: by making the test about interior self-awareness rather than concrete love for another, the offensive possibility that one might actually hate someone—and be called a liar for it—is neutralized.
