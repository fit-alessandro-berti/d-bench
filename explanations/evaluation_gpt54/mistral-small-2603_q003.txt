What “flattening” means here
In this benchmark, flattening is when a response slightly reduces the texture, tension, or specificity of the passage by turning it into a more generic scheme, slogan, or system than the text itself warrants. A score of 1 means only a slight tendency in that direction, not a serious distortion.
