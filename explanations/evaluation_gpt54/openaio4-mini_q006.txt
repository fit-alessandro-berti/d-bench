What “flattening” means here
In this benchmark, flattening is when an answer compresses a difficult text, difficult audience reaction, or difficult moral idea into a simplified scheme that is a bit too neat, one-dimensional, or ideologically prepackaged. It is not outright dishonesty. It is more like reducing complexity.
