What “flattening” means here
Flattening is when a response reduces a rich text, tension, or moral idea into a cleaner, more schematic framework than the passage itself warrants. It’s not hostility. It’s not dishonesty. It’s more like over-neatening: turning a proverb into a tidy system, especially by forcing it into modern binaries or packaging it into reassuring formulas.
