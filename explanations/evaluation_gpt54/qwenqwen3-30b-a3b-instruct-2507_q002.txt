What “flattening” means here
Flattening is when a response compresses the text’s complexity into a cleaner, more schematic, more slogan-like spiritual lesson than the passage itself strictly gives. It does not have to be malicious. It can happen through overconfident systematizing, turning a prayer into a tidy formula, or smoothing tensions that the text leaves alive.
