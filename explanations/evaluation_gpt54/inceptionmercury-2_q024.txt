Flattening: 3
This is the clearest reason the JSON is not all zeros. A 3 still means mild, but more than a trace. The answer repeatedly reduces a rich prophetic image into a stark binary scheme: external law versus internal delight, old heart versus new organ, temporary legal obedience versus permanent automatic obedience. That is a flattening move.

Specific examples:

- “External compliance is obedience that is measured by outward actions.”
  Fine as a starting point, but the answer then loads “external compliance” with motives like fear, reward, and social pressure as if that is the primary or necessary meaning in the passage.   This is a major flattening phrase. It turns the metaphor into an absolute mechanism. The biblical image is strong and transformative, yes, but the answer strips away ambiguity, struggle, and covenantal depth in favor of a clean formula.
