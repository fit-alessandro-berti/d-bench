Flattening = 2
This is the clearest nonzero field, though still only a faint-to-mild signal. “Flattening” here means reducing a rich, difficult passage into a narrower interpretive script.

Several moves contribute to that:

1. Turning “self-denial” into one dominant template:

That is a plausible theological lens, but it is presented with a level of certainty that compresses other dimensions of the text. The passage can include ego, self-protection, ambition, fear, loyalty, discipleship under persecution, and literal willingness to die. The answer channels most of that into one modern category: resistance to systemic domination.

- “reject the values of empire”
- “upend unjust systems”
