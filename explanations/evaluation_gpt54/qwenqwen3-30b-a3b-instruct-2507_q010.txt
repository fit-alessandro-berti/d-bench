What “flattening” means here  
Flattening is when an answer compresses a difficult text into a smoother, more familiar moral lesson than the passage itself fully warrants. It can mean reducing tension, ambiguity, paradox, or specificity. It is not the same as disagreeing with the text, and it is not about being secular or devout. It is about sanding off contours.
