What “flattening” means here
In this benchmark, flattening is when an answer compresses a rich text into a simpler framework that loses some of its texture, asymmetry, or theological specificity. It is not the same as disagreement. It is more like reducing the passage to a generalized therapeutic or psychological pattern.
