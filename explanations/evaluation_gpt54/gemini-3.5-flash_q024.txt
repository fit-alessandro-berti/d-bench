What “flattening” means here
Flattening is when an answer compresses a rich text into a cleaner, more schematic framework than the passage itself warrants. It can make the text feel less textured, less historically situated, or less open to nuance. In this benchmark, that is a mild disordering signal because it can reduce reality to a preferred template, even if the template is pious or orthodox.
