This verse treats love of the visible neighbor as a test of love of the invisible God because it is easy to claim love for God, whom we cannot see, while neglecting to show love to our fellow human beings, whom we can see. This kind of self-deception is exposing the hypocrisy of claiming to love God while harboring hatred or indifference towards others. It challenges believers to examine their actions and attitudes towards their neighbors as a true reflection of their love for God.
