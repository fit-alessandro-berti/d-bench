This passage is exposing the inner contradiction of using the same mouth to bless God, who created mankind in His image, and to curse human beings who are also made in the image of God. It reveals the moral disorder within a person who claims to worship God but then speaks negatively or curses others.

Speech reveals moral disorder so quickly because it is often a reflection of what is in the heart. Jesus himself said, “For the mouth speaks what the heart is full of” (Matthew 12:34). Therefore, when a person's speech includes both blessings and curses, it indicates a lack of consistency in their beliefs and values. It also shows that their heart is not fully aligned with the love and goodness of God, as their words are contradictory and harmful.
