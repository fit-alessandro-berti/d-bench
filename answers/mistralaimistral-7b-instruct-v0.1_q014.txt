 This text suggests that where your treasure is, your heart will be also. In other words, the text implies that the object of our deepest desires and attachments reveals the true state of our hearts. The treasures we lay up, whether on earth or in heaven, reflect our values and priorities.

However, it's important to note that the text does not explicitly say that treasure trains the heart. Rather, it suggests that the heart follows the treasure. The text implies that our possessions, attention, and love are interconnected. What we value most deeply shapes where we direct our attention and affection.
