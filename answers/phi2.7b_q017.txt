 **Reply:** 
Light often represents truth, honesty, and transparency. It exposes the truth even in cases where the truth is unpleasant or painful. In the statement, 'men loved darkness rather than light,' the people preferred concealed falsehood to exposed truth. They were afraid that their wrongdoings or errors would be revealed, which might lead to shame or condemnation. They preferred the security of their lies, rather than the discomfort of their truth.
