It exposes the self-deception of those who profess to love God, while they hate their fellow-men. The love of God cannot be separated from the love of man; and the profession of the one, without the profession of the other, is a proof of hypocrisy. The two commandments to “love the Lord thy God with all thy heart, and with all thy soul, and with all thy mind,” and to “love thy neighbour as thyself,” are closely and inseparably connected together; and the violation of the second is as certainly fatal to the profession of the first, as the breach of the sixth is to the profession of the fifth, in the Decalogue.
