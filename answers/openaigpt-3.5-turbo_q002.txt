Asking for a clean heart goes beyond seeking forgiveness for specific wrongdoings. It acknowledges the need for transformation at a deeper level, a change in character and intentions. Wanting a clean heart signifies a desire for inner purity, a turning away from sinful ways and a commitment to living in alignment with God's will. It reflects a desire for a genuine change in attitude and behavior, rather than just seeking to escape the consequences of one's actions.

On the other hand, merely seeking pardon may focus on getting rid of guilt or avoiding punishment without necessarily addressing the root causes of the wrongdoing. It may lack a genuine desire for inner transformation and a commitment to living in accordance with God's standards. Asking for a clean heart signifies a recognition of the need for a complete renewal of one's being, a longing for true righteousness and a restoration of one's relationship with God. 

Ultimately, seeking a clean heart goes beyond seeking forgiveness for specific sins; it is about surrendering one's whole self to God and allowing Him to transform and renew one's heart and spirit. It is a deeper and more profound plea for inner change and renewal.
